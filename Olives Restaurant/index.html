Our Story

Fusion Cuisine,
Ugandan Heart.

Born in Naguru, Olives bridges the world's great culinary traditions — the slow-cooked richness of Northern India, the rustic elegance of Italian kitchens — with East African hospitality that turns every meal into a memory.

Every plate is crafted by our team of international chefs using locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients from Uganda's finest markets.

  • 🌿 Farm-to-table sourcing from Ugandan farmers
  • 🍷 Curated wine & cocktail pairing menu
  • 👨‍🍳 International chefs, proudly local ingredients
